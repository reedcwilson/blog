from dull to delicious

30 January 2014

Family history work has never seemed like a faddish or cool thing to be into. I remember creating a four generation pedigree chart in elementary school to help me become acquainted with my ancestors. It was onerous and I had no interest in it. In general, youth stigmatize genealogy work as a boring activity to avoid at all costs. While it used to be lifeless, the Internet makes family history work easier and more exciting. You can now have interesting pictures and animations as you search for your ancestors. Websites like ancestry.com or familysearch.org have made it easy and engaging to search through endless records. You may even find yourself losing track of time and realizing that you have actually enjoyed yourself where you may have otherwise fallen asleep from the tedium that accompanies dusty pages and dull charts.

Reed Wilson
