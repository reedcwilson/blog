piece of your mind

02 March 2014

Now that anyone can share their opinion and have it shared with the world, we can now see that the world is filled with rotten and adversarial people who disagree on any and every imaginable topic. Of course it isn’t true that everyone out there is argumentative but after reading some public forums or the comments on articles it sometimes feels that way. In an age where anyone can speak their mind and be heard by the public, I would think that now, more than ever, people would strive to put on their best face. The derisive comments that exist on so many websites may stay there for years to come. Instead of trying to formulate the sharpest and most biting remark to aim at someone’s publicly shared thoughts or views, it would be refreshing to see the more pleasant parts of people’s personalities for a change.

Reed Wilson
